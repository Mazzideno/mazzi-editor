# 🎨 MazziGroup Post Editor
MazziGroup Post Editor - Offline PWA
**A sleek, 100% offline Progressive Web App (PWA) for creating beautiful social media posts, quote graphics, and more. No Photoshop required!**

🔗 **Live App:** [https://mazzideno.github.io/mazzi-editor/](https://mazzideno.github.io/mazzi-editor/)

---

## ✨ Features

- **📱 100% Offline (PWA):** Works without an internet connection. Install it on your phone or PC and use it anywhere.
- **🔒 Secure Local Auth:** User registration, login, and "Remember Me" functionality stored securely in the browser.
- ** Terms & Conditions Gate:** First-time users must agree to the terms before accessing the editor.
- **🖼️ Canvas-Based Editor:** Powerful yet simple drag-and-drop style interface for designing posts.
- **⚡ Pre-built Templates:** Quick-start templates for Faith, Prayer, Worship, and Remnant quotes.
- **📤 Easy Export:** Download your creations instantly as high-quality **JPG** or **PNG** files.
- **🎨 Full Customization:** Change fonts, colors, text alignment, shadows, and upload custom backgrounds.
- **🌙 Premium Dark UI:** Beautiful, modern dark theme with smooth animations and orange brand accents.

---

## ️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** (Semantic structure)
- **CSS3** (Custom properties, Flexbox/Grid, Glassmorphism, Animations)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (Canvas API, LocalStorage, DOM manipulation)
- **Service Workers** (For offline PWA capabilities)

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Visit the App:** Open [https://mazzideno.github.io/mazzi-editor/](https://mazzideno.github.io/mazzi-editor/) in your browser.
2. **Accept Terms:** Read and agree to the Terms & Conditions on your first visit.
3. **Create an Account:** Register a local username and password (stored only on your device).
4. **Start Creating:** Pick a template, upload a background, edit your text, and hit Export!
5. **Install (Optional):** 
   - **PC:** Click the "Install" icon in your browser's address bar.
   - **Mobile:** Tap your browser menu and select "Add to Home Screen".
